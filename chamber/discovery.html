Murray History

Historic Downtown Residential District Includes 185 historic structures that extend from Center St. to Atwood and Glen Street and from 4800 S to Vine Street with a small extension on Vine Street to 451 E Vine. 13 of the 185 buildings were researched in more detail to illustrate the architectural history of the residential area between 1870 and 1950.

Events

July 4, 2023 Fun Days Parade Lineup of entries begins at 7:30 a.m. at the Northwest corner of Fashion Place Mall (6100 S State Street) Travels from 6100 S and State north to Vine St (turns east on Vine Street to 330 E entrance of Murray Park; ends at Hillcrest Jr. High) Parade entries are judged and awards given for the following categories Special Interest/Antique Vehicle (pre 1970) Business/Commercial Equestrian/Animal Civic/Royalty/Political/Float

News

Murray City Fire Department to Participate in National Emergency Medical Services Week May 21-27, 2023 has been designated as National Emergency Medical Service (EMS) Week. National EMS week honors the lifesaving care EMS providers offer nationwide, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. This year we will be hosting for the public our Second Annual Pancake Breakfast to kick off EMS week, at Station 81 (4848 S Box Elder St) on May 20th from 8 a.m. - 10 a.m.
