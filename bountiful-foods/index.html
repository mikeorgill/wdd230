Bountiful takes special orders, as well as offers special holiday menu orders which are available for pick-up or delivery. Nationwide next-day and two day shipping also available.

Bountiful Foods, a locally renowned baking and catering company created by Chef/Owner Denise Assad, has been serving the SoCal community for over a decade. At Bountiful Foods, Denise’s mission is to create hearty, homemade food so her customers don’t have to.

Please visit us on Saturday mornings at the Laguna Beach Farmers’ Market.
